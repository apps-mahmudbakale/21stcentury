The 21st Century Entrepreneurs Hub

is a platform whereby entrepreneurs get connected with arrays of businesses and business options, consultants, mentors and Guides with a view to deepening their individual knowledge and understanding of the respective industry their businesses depend upon through a deliberate and organized activity-based programs. The platform promotes constructive engagement, collaborations, networking and connection of entrepreneurs in new and modern ways of doing business. Opportunities are abounds to leveraging robust and esteemed interactions with public agencies, resource personalities, researchers as well as knowledge of the requirements of the communities in relation to consumer behaviour in order to provide innovative solutions to the ever changing communal and individual demands in real modern world.
The 21st Century Entrepreneurs Hub was founded in 2016 . It is intended to serve as an incubating theatre to hatch entrepreneurs for start-up businesses that will inculcate guidance and requisite skills on handling the intricacies and complexities of risk laden attributes in venture capital businesses and their sources of finance. We have four target audiences;

- The society is full of investive minds with coulorful and feasible business ideas who's drive are hampered by dearth of capital and mentoring for a successful business take up and subsequent profitability.
- Those with an already established business will be brought together under one umbrella for the purposes of coaching, mentoring and Guidance on how to turn around their SMEs into conglomerates that can compete in an emerging world business order.
- It serves as a linkage forum with a compendium of business ideas and options in a financial match making function with arrays of financial institutions, development partners and donors with investive funds to assist established and start up businesses e.g Angel Investors Tony elumelu, Dangote and Danjuma Foundations.
- And the young upcoming ones in the secondary schools and tertiary institutions; mentorship programs will be conducted regularly on how to be creative from early age in ways that yield into entrepreneurship mindsets.
