Sokoto 2018 Expo & Career Fair

The Sokoto 2018 Expo and Career Fair will be home to the 21st Century Entrepreneurs Hub's Marketplace, where more than 200+ entrepreneurs and start-ups are expected to showcase their products and services and have the opportunity to expand their client base, networking, gain exposure to investors and so much more. The exhibition and trade fair will hold on 30th June 2018, while the conference and technical workshops will hold on 7th July 2018.
